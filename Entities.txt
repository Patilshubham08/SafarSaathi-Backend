1. The Core Entities (Standard)
These remain exactly as we discussed.

1. Users: user_id, name, email, role, password.

2. Packages: pkg_id, name, price, vendor_id, image_url.

3. Trips: trip_id, customer_id, package_id, name, status.

4. Bookings: booking_id, trip_id, status, date.

5. Transportation
Instead of distinct arrival/departure/seat fields, just use generic descriptions.

trans_id (PK)

trip_id (FK)

type (String: "Flight", "Train")

details (String: "Flight AI-202, Seat 4A, 10:00 AM")

price (Decimal)




6. Accommodation
accom_id (PK)

trip_id (FK)

hotel_name (String)

address (String: Just City/Area is fine)

price (Decimal)




7. Destinations
dest_id (PK)

trip_id (FK)

name (String: "Eiffel Tower")

description (String: "Visit in the evening")
